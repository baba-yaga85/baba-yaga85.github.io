ℹ️ About This Tool

Some music players require specific settings for FLAC files. If your FiiO SNOWSKY ECHO Mini fails to play your audio, the internal block size is likely the cause. This tool automatically adjusts your music files to a block size of 4608. This size is the standard choice for most high-resolution audio players.

The software handles the heavy lifting while keeping your music quality intact. It preserves your album artwork and all metadata tags during the process. You get a perfect copy of your library that works on your portable player without extra effort.

  • At least 100MB of free disk space
  • Basic knowledge of file folders
